ABOUT HAVE

MISSION

HAVE deploys unique solutions that prevent and alleviate pressing social issues in Vietnam by utilizing innovations and disciplinary practices across different engineering domains, which is only possible due to the invaluable support from the wider Vietnamese society.

VISION

With a network of devoted engineers and compassionate donors, HAVE aims to bring dynamic engineering/technology applications to solve complex real-life social issues. The issues are specifically chosen at the crossroad where the potential nonexistence of long-term profits prevents innovative enterprises from tackling major social issues.

The range of social issues is neither limited in scope nor nature; but should only be endeavored once HAVE determines it has sufficient capacity in domain knowledge, engineering expertise, and funding to pursue a solution to its conclusive outcome.

Humanity

HAVE exists to promote humanism by pursuing improvements to the quality of life of people in Vietnam. HAVE believes a society is only as healthy and prosperous as its most disadvantaged people. To that extent HAVE aims to put the wellbeing and fulfillment of its internal people at the forefront of every decision it is to make. HAVE hopes that its pursuit of humanism will inspire and lead a movement where deep expertise in any given field is leveraged to contribute to a Vietnamese society built upon mutual understanding, cooperation, happiness and peace.

HavePaws

HavePaws is the first project undertaken by HAVE. This project aims to alleviate the negative social impacts caused by the Dog & Cat Meat Trade (DCMT) industry in Vietnam and its subsequent fallout effects.

ISSUES

In Vietnam, the Dog & Cat Meat Trade (DCMT) results in numerous legal, medical and safety risks that seriously affect the community

Public Health

DCMT creates an ideal environment for the spread of existing diseases (like rabies) and the emergence of new zoonotic diseases (similar to the COVID-19 outbreak).

Social Order & Security

Pet thefts occur in many areas, rural and urban alike, sparking outrage and distress among the community. This leads to unwanted violence fueled by anger from owners towards thieves, resulting in serious (sometimes even fatal) consequences.

Animal Abuse and Tourism

On the economic front, the tourism industry in Vietnam accounts for nearly 10% of the GDP, creating more than 6 million direct jobs and more than 2 million indirect jobs. Solution

We created the HavePaws mobile app as a solution to the aforementioned issues. The app aims alleviate negative consequences from pet theft by reconnecting lost pets with owners, push for higher transparency and better food safety in DCMT, and contribute to building a more civilized and tourist-friendly national image of Vietnam in the eyes of international friends and visitors.

Utilize image processing AI in recognizing and automatically matching images of lost dogs and cats with found reports to help owners and finders connect as quickly as possible.

A smart map feature to showcase pet-related stores and services, helping businesses to increase their market reach while allowing owners to easily find services and products that best match their needs.

A standardized adoption process for rescued dogs and cats with software support in the filtering/profile review stage. This aims to save time and costs for rescue organizations, expand the reach of possible adopters, as well as increase transparency amongst parties.
